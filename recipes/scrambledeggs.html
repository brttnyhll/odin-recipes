Scrambled Eggs

Description

Eggs that are scrambled. You crack them then scramble them up. Make sure to cook them in a pan. No soggy eggs here.

If you get the eggs to room temperture first they won't stick to the pan. Isn't that neat.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Pan
  • Cheese
  1. Crack the egg into a bowl and scramble it
  2. Pour the scrambled eggs into the hot pan
  3. Take the eggs out of the pan once they're solid
