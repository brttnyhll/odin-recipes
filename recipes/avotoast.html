Avocado Toast

Toast with some avocado on it

Really in depth analysis about avocado toast and why it's good. Very simple and tasty. My dead grandma made avocado toast in 1920 for my grandpa. It's not just millenials.

You can't afford a house if you have too much avocado toast. Avocados are very expensive. Especially at Whole Foods. Like double the price of anywhere else.

Ingredients

  • Avocado
  • Toast
  • Garlic Powder
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Smash the avocadoes in a bowl
  2. Add some garlic, lime, salt, pepper to avocadoes
  3. Spread the smashed avocadoes on the toast
